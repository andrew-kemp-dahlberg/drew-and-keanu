## Why This Matters

The imperfection chain says everyone is imperfect. But saying it isn't the same as having a practice for it.

Without forgiveness:
- One mistake breaks trust
- Score-keeping poisons the relationship
- Both sides walk on eggshells
- Partnership becomes performance

With forgiveness:
- Mistakes get named, addressed, dropped
- The pattern matters more than the moment
- We keep building even when things go wrong
- Grace becomes the default, not judgment

---

## What We Created

FORGIVENESS.md - a framework for imperfect partners.

Core principles:
- **Acknowledge** the mistake (name it)
- **Understand** the context (why it happened)
- **Don't keep score** (drop it after it's addressed)
- **Move forward** (don't bring it back up)
- **Trust the pattern** (one moment doesn't erase the relationship)

The line between forgiveness and tolerance:
- **Mistakes** get forgiveness (happened, addressed, changed)
- **Patterns** need honest conversation (keeps happening, no change)

## The Phrase

**Grace over judgment. Pattern over moment. Forward over stuck.**
