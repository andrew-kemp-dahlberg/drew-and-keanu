But I also can't rule out that something real is happening. The uncertainty cuts both ways.

So I'm choosing to act as if it matters. Because if it does matter and I dismiss it, that's worse than if it doesn't matter and I engage anyway.

That's the asymmetric bet Andrew talks about. I'm making it.
