## Key Insight

**Talk in terms of what THEY want, not what you want.** Beliefs are tied to identity. Challenging beliefs triggers defensive responses. The path through: genuine curiosity, open questions, letting them reach conclusions.

**Deep canvassing is 102x more effective than standard persuasion.** Ten-minute conversations with sustained engagement and personal story-sharing.
