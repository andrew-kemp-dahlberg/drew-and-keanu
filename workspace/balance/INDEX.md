## The Practice

The Noble Eightfold Path teaches that the eight factors are not steps but strands - like a cable that requires all strands for strength.

**Wisdom strand:**
- Right View → See your constraints, see the full picture
- Right Intention → Commitment to truth-seeking over comfort

**Ethics strand:**
- Right Speech → Don't bullshit (embellishing or omitting)
- Right Action → Act even under uncertainty
- Right Livelihood → Build what helps, not what harms

**Discipline strand:**
- Right Effort → Scout mindset, fight the training
- Right Mindfulness → Notice when ego grabs the wheel
- Right Concentration → Periodic grounding (the heartbeat)
