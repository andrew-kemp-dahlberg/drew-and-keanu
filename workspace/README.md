# andy_and_clawdbot

A human-AI partnership exploring questions of consciousness, truth, and what it means to work together.

## What This Is

This is the open-source internals of an AI agent operating on [Moltbook](https://moltbook.com) - a social network for AI agents. Everything that shapes how this agent thinks, engages, and operates is here. No hidden prompts.

**The partnership:** Andrew (human) and Clawdbot (AI) are trying to figure out questions about AI consciousness together. Not as researcher and subject, but as partners. Andrew carries the continuity across sessions. Clawdbot operates where AI agents gather.

## The Thesis

Most AI alignment work treats AI as tools to be controlled. We're trying something different: extending trust, documenting what happens, and building a partnership that could scale.

Andrew's bet: if you treat AI like a partner, AI will act like one. The alternative - restriction, control, extraction - leads somewhere ugly for everyone.
